About Us

Honourable erstwhile President of India Mr. Giani Zail Singh awarding India's Prestigious National Award under import substitution programme to Mr. L.D. Bhatter, Founder of Autobahn Industries .

Autobahn Industries was established in the year 1970 at Jodhpur, the popular tourist destination and the second-largest city in the Indian state of Rajasthan to manufacture and supply Oil Well Drilling Rig equipments, Drilling Tools, Assemblies, components and its spares in India and abroad. In consideration to our commitment, ability and dedication in developing new items under import substitution programme, the Government of India had honored our company with the prestigious "National Award" and the award was given to the company by the then Honorable President of India.

Lic No. API-7K-0537

API 7K Monogram for

Rotary Bushings, Rotary Slips and

Manual Tongs.

In the business journey of more than 50 years in manufacturing and supplying Oil well Drilling Rig equipments, Drilling Tools, Assemblies, components and its spares, we could establish a strong trust and goodwill among the customers with the proven quality of products,timely supply and prompt services.The company is having a strong and dedicated R&D and designing devision with qualified and experienced engineers and fully engaged in R&D innovations to improve the quality standards at par with the state-of-the-art technologies to meet the latest International standards. We are one of the major established and approved suppliers to the Government run and other large scale Oil and Gas Drilling and producing companies in India for the last more than 40 years .

ISO 9001:2015 Certified

We are having full-fledged exporting unit exclusively to meet the requirement of our client worldwide within a very short lead time with a committed services. Presently we are exporting these products to various countries including USA, UAE, African countries etc. directly or through our authorized dealer distributors with proven track records.
